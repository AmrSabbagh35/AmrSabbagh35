## How I think about software

- Architecture is a series of bets. I'd rather make them on purpose than by default.
- Shipping beats perfect. Then I iterate on what users *actually* do — not what I assumed.
- A B2B + B2C product is two products wearing one logo. Respect that or it tears.
- Bilingual UX (AR/EN, RTL) isn't a setting you flip at the end. It's the design.
- I'd rather understand *why* we're building it than be handed a ticket.
